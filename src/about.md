 <h3>Your partner in traffic matters</h3>

 <p>The team of verkehrsteiner AG supports you in dealing with traffic planning issues and developing needs-based mobility solutions. We collect and analyze the traffic on your road network for you using a wide variety of methods and provide you with the basics for further planning. One of our core competencies lies in video-based traffic analysis. Our range of services also includes offers of integral traffic and urban planning. We offer you ideas and solutions with the aim of implementing sustainable mobility that ensures the coexistence of all road users and guarantees a high quality of the surrounding area. We bring a lot of experience in the work for school safety as well as the implementation of Tempo 30 zones and meeting zones.</p>

 <h3>We plan the future of mobility</h3>

 <p>Based on the traffic analyses or other existing foundations developed by us, we will work with you to find the appropriate solution for your needs. We pursue the goal of creating a safe and comfortable transport facility for all road users, in which coexistence and mutual consideration are a matter of course.</p>

 <p>Based on our experience, we are also specialized in the analysis and planning of bicycle transport facilities. We pursue the approach of "Shared Space" in the context of the implementation of meeting zones, speed 30 zones and flat crossings. The aim of our planning is to make public space more livable for all people and to be able to satisfactorily handle the mobility needs of all.</p>
