<p>The team of verkehrsteiner AG supports you in dealing with traffic planning issues and developing needs-based mobility solutions. We collect and analyze the traffic on your road network for you using a wide variety of methods and provide you with the basics for further planning. One of our core competencies lies in video-based traffic analysis. Our range of services also includes offers of integral traffic and urban planning. We offer you ideas and solutions with the aim of implementing sustainable mobility that ensures the coexistence of all road users and guarantees a high quality of the surrounding area. We bring a lot of experience in the work for school safety as well as the implementation of Tempo 30 zones and meeting zones. One of our core competencies is video-based traffic analysis. For example, driving relationships can be mapped and qualitative statements about traffic processes can be made. We have a large number of side radars for traffic counts and speed measurements.</p>

<p>Based on the traffic analyses or other existing foundations developed by us, we will work with you to find the appropriate solution for your needs. We pursue the goal of creating a safe and comfortable transport facility for all road users, in which coexistence and mutual consideration are a matter of course. An important concern for us is the safety of the way to school. We bring a wealth of experience with us through numerous realized projects and research work in this field. Based on our experience, we are also specialized in the analysis and planning of bicycle transport facilities. We pursue the approach of "Shared Space" in the context of the implementation of meeting zones, speed 30 zones and flat crossings. The aim of our planning is to make public space more livable for all people and to be able to satisfactorily handle the mobility needs of all.</p>

<p>By means of mobility management, the transport demand can be influenced or organized. Through strategies, action concepts and measures from the areas of information, communication, motivation and coordination, traffic users are offered the opportunity to rethink and change their mobility behavior and their attitudes to mobility. At the same time, demand is influenced by a corresponding supply. As a company, municipality or builder, you assume responsibility for the traffic you cause. The goal is to make the traffic arising from the mobility needs efficient, resource-saving and environmentally and socially compatible.</p>

<p>We prepare a site analysis for you and work with you to develop a mobility strategy with an extensive mobility concept and action planning for your company, your area or the entire municipal area. Of course, we also take over the necessary monitoring and controlling.</p>
